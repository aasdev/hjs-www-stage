About the foundation

Halimbi Jamal Shaikh (HJS) Educational Funding was established to promote education among economically and socially disadvantaged Indian Minority communities and their children who are unable to pay fees and pursue their education to improve their economic and educational conditions. These communities are economically disadvantage groups who hail from Kumta and neighboring villages. Kumta Taluk and its neighboring villages are located on the west coast of Karnataka 500 miles south of Mumbai and 100 miles south of Goa. It is known for beautiful beaches and long coast lines and home for many fishing villages. These minorities come from Muslim and non-Muslim backgrounds, and are educationally deprived compared with their counterpart majority communities.
