Funds collected by HJS Educational Funding are used to provide financial assistance to socially and economically disadvantaged students in primary and secondary schools in coastal areas in India. We also provide scholarships to poor students pursuing higher education in India to improve their prospects for better job opportunities.

Recipients of Scholarships will be required to submit progress reports of their studies, including their attested grade transcripts.
